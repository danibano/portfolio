About Me

Hello! I'm Dani. I am 23 years old, I was born and raised in Minnesota. I went to Saint Paul Conservatory for the Performing Artists and graduated in 2017. I've been dancing for over 15 years, training in many styles including ballet, jazz, tap, modern, contemporary, and breaking. I currently dance with BRKFST Dance Company, a collabrative breaking group. This past summer we had the chance to make a piece with the Minnesota Orchestra to Beethovan's Grosse Fuge.
